Afternoon Tea

Indulge in an authentic Victorian afternoon tea experience, with our wide selection of teas, champagnes and pastries. We also offer afternoon tea for children and guests with dietary restrictions. Please see our Menu page for more information. Join us for a performance by the Doughnut String Quartet, every afternoon from 3pm.

Hours

Monday to Friday: 9am - 5pm
Weekends: 8am - 6pm
