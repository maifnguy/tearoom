Afternoon Tea

Traditional Afternoon Tea$40

Indulge in our selection of finger sandwiches, and warm raisin and plain scones from our bakery served with homemade strawberry jam, a seasonal jam and Cornish clotted cream. Also served with French pastries, and a choice of our specially selected teas.

Champagne Afternoon Tea$55

In addition to our offerings for our traditional afternoon tea, choose between Laurent-Perrier NV Champagne, Laurent-Perrier Cuvée Rosé Champagne or a glass of Dom Pérignon Vintage Champagne for a special occasion.

Royal Afternoon Tea$60

Indulge in the ultimate afternoon tea experience, with our selection of finger sandwiches, scones served with jam and Cornish clotted cream, French pastries, cake, bottomless tea, and sorbet.

Childen's Afternoon Tea$30

A selection of finger sandwiches, biscuits, cake, icecream and chocolate milk for the children!

Gentlemen's Afternoon Tea$50

Our Gentlemen’s Afternoon Tea offers steak sandwiches, sliders, smoked rasher scones and whiskey chocolate truffles with beer or whiskey. Also suitable for ladies looking for an alternative to the Traditional Afternoon Tea.

Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea$45

We offer finger sandwiches on gluten free bread, coconut bites, gluten free scones, and sorbet with our selection of teas.

Tea

Royal English

An exquisite blend of Kenyan, malty Assam and the fine flavor of Ceylon Orange Pekoe.

Earl Grey

A legendary blend of Chinese twisted leaf black tea and bergamot, silky smooth with a delightful aroma.

Rooibos Red

Caffeine free, rich in vitamin C, mineral salts and anti-oxidants with a warm red colour.

Passion Fruit

A wonderful taste of apple, hibiscus and sweet orange peel blended with passion fruit and orange segments.

Orange Pekoe

A whole leaf black tea from Sri Lanka producing a rich amber color with full flavor and scented aroma.

Dragon Pearls

A silver needle tea from Fujian, China, hand-rolled and delicately scented with Jasmine flowers.

Camomile Flowers

A calming and delicate infusion of natural camomile with a floral aroma and sweet, soothing flavor.

Masala Chai

A unique blend of Assam and Chai spices that has been crafted in-house by our Master of Tea.

Lemon Verbena

Produced from Verveine leaves,an elegant, sparkling lemon tasting tisane. This herbal tea is the ultimate digestif.

Formosa Oolong

A semi-fermented tea from Taiwan, attractive amber hue, slightly nutty taste yet fully developed aroma.

Darjeeling First Flush

The Champagne of teas from the Himalayan foothills creating a light amber color and a delicate muscatel aroma.

Rose Congou

A black tea from Guangdong, China, enriched with rose petals giving a gentle, light rose aroma to the tea.
